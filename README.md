# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📊 Project Overview

The **Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze pizza sales performance and provide meaningful business insights. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor revenue, customer purchasing behavior, sales trends, and product performance using interactive visualizations and KPIs.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

A pizza restaurant generates thousands of transactions every month. Management requires an interactive dashboard to monitor overall business performance, identify top-selling and low-performing products, analyze customer purchasing patterns, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze overall pizza sales performance.
* Track important business KPIs.
* Identify best-selling and worst-selling pizzas.
* Analyze daily and monthly sales trends.
* Understand sales by pizza category and size.
* Support business decisions through interactive dashboards.

---

# 📂 Dataset Information

* **Dataset:** Pizza Sales Dataset
* **Format:** CSV
* **Visualization Tool:** Microsoft Power BI
* **Data Modeling:** Power Query & DAX

---

# 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* 💰 Total Revenue
* 🛒 Total Orders
* 🍕 Total Pizzas Sold
* 💵 Average Order Value
* 📦 Average Pizzas per Order
